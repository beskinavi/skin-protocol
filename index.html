Pills & vitamins

—

Supplement timing

Iron — on an empty stomach. Avoid coffee, tea, dairy, or calcium for 2 hours before and after.

D3 — with a fatty meal (avocado, nuts, cheese, olive oil). Poor absorption on an empty stomach.

Magnesium — in the evening, supports sleep. Not in the morning.

Sertraline — take at the same time daily. Time of day is not critical.

Photo session

Standard protocol

Morning, before washing. 5 shots in consistent light. No beauty mode, no HDR, no flash.

  1. Frontal — face straight to camera, ~30–40 cm
  2. Left profile 45° — turn head right
  3. Right profile 45° — turn head left
  4. Forehead close-up — ~15–20 cm from forehead
  5. T-zone close-up — nose + perinasal + cheeks

Save as YYYY-MM-DD_name.jpg. Then log the session below.
